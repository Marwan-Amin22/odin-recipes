Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved dish made with a soft base, rich sauce, and melty cheese. It's baked until golden and crisp, with toppings that blend into a flavorful mix. Simple, customizable, and always delicious, pizza is a favorite for everyone.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Add pepperoni slices evenly across the pizza.
  6. Brush the crust edges with olive oil.
  7. Bake for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  8. Remove, slice, and enjoy warm.
