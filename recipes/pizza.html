Pizza

Description

Pizza comes in countless styles, each with its own twist, but here we're diving into the classic pepperoni pizza. It starts with a soft, golden crust layered with tangy sauce and gooey cheese. Topped with perfectly crisped pepperoni, it's the kind of pizza that never goes out of style. Simple, bold, and undeniably delicious, this is the ultimate crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Add pepperoni slices evenly across the pizza.
  6. Brush the crust edges with olive oil.
  7. Bake for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  8. Remove, slice, and enjoy warm.
